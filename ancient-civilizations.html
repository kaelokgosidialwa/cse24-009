    Ancient Civilizations

    Discover the rise and fall of ancient societies such as Mesopotamia, Egypt, Greece, and Rome, and their impact on modern civilization.

    Key Topics Covered

    This course provides a historical survey of early human civilizations. Students explore the cultural, political, and technological advancements of ancient societies and their enduring legacies.

    • Early Human Societies
      • Hunter-gatherer communities
      • The Neolithic Revolution
    • River Valley Civilizations
      • Mesopotamia, Egypt, Indus Valley, and China
      • Governance, religion, and daily life
    • Classical Civilizations
      • Ancient Greece and Rome
      • Contributions to philosophy, art, and science
    • Comparative Analysis
      • Trade networks and conflict
      • Legacy and influence on modern society

    Learning Outcomes

    • Understand the development of early civilizations
    • Analyze primary and secondary historical sources
    • Draw connections between past and present cultures
