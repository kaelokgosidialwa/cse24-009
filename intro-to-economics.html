    Intro To Economics

    Explore the principles of microeconomics and macroeconomics, including supply and demand, market structures, fiscal and monetary policy, and global trade.

    Key Topics Covered

    This course introduces the fundamental principles of microeconomics and macroeconomics. Students will learn how economies function at both individual and societal levels, focusing on supply and demand, market structures, economic indicators, and the role of government policy.

    • Basic Economic Concepts
      • Scarcity and opportunity cost
      • Factors of production
      • Economic systems
    • Microeconomics
      • Supply and demand analysis
      • Elasticity of demand and supply
      • Market structures: perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly
    • Macroeconomics
      • GDP, inflation, and unemployment
      • Fiscal and monetary policy
      • Business cycles and economic growth
    • Government and the Economy
      • Taxation and government spending
      • Regulation and public goods
      • International trade and globalization

    Learning Outcomes

    • Understand basic economic terminology and models
    • Analyze market behavior using supply and demand frameworks
    • Interpret economic indicators and policy impacts
    • Apply economic reasoning to real-world problems
