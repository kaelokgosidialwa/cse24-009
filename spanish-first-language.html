    Spanish First Language

    Enhance fluency in Spanish through reading, writing, grammar, and cultural exploration designed for native speakers.

    Key Topics Covered

    This course is designed for native Spanish speakers to strengthen their command of the language. Emphasis is placed on advanced grammar, writing styles, literature, and oral communication.

    • Advanced Grammar and Syntax
      • Complex sentence structures
      • Verb conjugations in various tenses
    • Literature and Analysis
      • Classic and modern Spanish literature
      • Critical reading and interpretation
    • Composition and Writing
      • Formal and informal writing styles
      • Essay structure and argument development
    • Oral Communication
      • Public speaking and discussion
      • Pronunciation and fluency

    Learning Outcomes

    • Develop advanced reading and writing skills in Spanish
    • Analyze literary texts and express critical opinions
    • Enhance oral fluency and presentation skills
