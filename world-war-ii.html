      World War Two

      Analyze the causes, major events, and consequences of World War II, including key battles, political strategies, and global outcomes.

      Key Topics Covered

      This course examines the global conflict of World War II, including its causes, key events, and far-reaching consequences. Students will explore military strategies, political ideologies, and human experiences during the war.

      • Causes of World War II
        • Treaty of Versailles and economic turmoil
        • Rise of fascism and expansionist policies
      • Major Theaters of War
        • Europe, Pacific, North Africa
        • Key battles and turning points
      • The Holocaust
        • Anti-Semitism and Nazi ideology
        • Genocide and resistance
      • Aftermath and Legacy
        • United Nations and international justice
        • Cold War origins

      Learning Outcomes

      • Understand the causes and course of World War II
      • Evaluate the impact on global politics and societies
      • Recognize the human cost and ethical dimensions of war
